Game:   Spaceship over terrain
Idea:   
        The player controls a spaceship across terrain

How to play:
-       The player starts at the starting position
-       Left click to make the spaceship move in a sharp direction
-       Right click causes the spaceship to speed through special terrain, with countdown
-       When the spacecraft collides, the game ends

Difficulty: The spacecraft will gradually accelerate, obstacles will increase in difficulty over time
